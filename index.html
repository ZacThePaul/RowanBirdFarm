Our Methodology

At Rowanbird Farm, we're committed to growing 100% natural food using regenerative practices. We practice "no-till farming", which involves minimizing disturbance to the soil's microbiology throughout the year because we're not just growing food, we're growing soil. Instead of using machinery, we rely on naturally occurring microbiology, such as insects and fungi, to loosen and aerate our soil. This not only helps the environment, but it also results in the highest quality food for our family and yours
